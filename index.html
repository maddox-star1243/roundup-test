How prices move
Log a drink → price spikes instantly
Every drink log bumps that drink +0.6–1.2. Active groups multiply upward moves up to 2.5×.
Inactivity bleeds prices down
Every 15s with no logs, a drink loses 0.4–0.7. Hold too long and your investment bleeds out.
Selling creates pressure
Selling shares drops price. Buy after a dump for a discount. Mean reversion pulls prices back to base overnight.
Your fantasy picks are highlighted
Drinks you drafted show a DRAFT tag. Logging your drafted drink boosts both your fantasy score and its stock price.
