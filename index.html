How prices move
Player buys → price moves up
Buying shares is the main way prices rise. One share creates a small shared bump, and larger trades are capped so prices do not explode.
Player sells → price moves down
Selling shares creates downward pressure. Prices do not randomly fall just because the app is open.
Drink logs create hype, not direct price jumps
Drink logs show demand, tonight volume, and fantasy activity, but they no longer directly spike the stock price.
Your fantasy picks are highlighted
Drinks you drafted show a DRAFT tag. Logging your drafted drink boosts fantasy score and hype, but stock prices only move from buy/sell trades.
